Membership for internationals in Denmark

Everything you need to settlesettle, work, grow and belong in Denmark.

InDe is the membership community for internationals making Denmark home, and the Danes who welcome them. We know the things that trip people up here, and we stand with you through them. Join free and you belong from day one. Add a level when you want deeper, one to one support.

What membership unlocks

Four ways InDe stands with you.

Building a life in a new country is not one task, it is many, and they arrive in waves. InDe is built in four pillars that follow your life here, from your first week to leading where you are. Each service shows the membership level that opens it, and whether it is live today or on the way.

Platinum Our flagship

Meet your Denmark Buddy.

Settling in is rarely about the paperwork. It is the hundred small things no one tells you. A Denmark Buddy is a real person who has done it before, matched to you, in your corner for the part that is hard to do alone. Part guide, part friend, part local ambassador.

  1. 1
    Get matched

    We pair you with a buddy near you who fits your situation, your field, and your language.

  2. 2
    Meet up

    A coffee, a walk, a call. They answer the awkward questions and show you how things really work here.

  3. 3
    Explore and settle

    A tour of your neighbourhood, help with a specific problem, and a friendly face through your first months.

How it works

Four steps. No paperwork wall.

  1. 1

    Join for free

    Sign up in a few minutes. No fee, no contract. You are a member from day one.

  2. 2

    Tell us what you need

    A short check-in points you to the right service, whether that is a contract review or a coffee.

  3. 3

    Get matched

  4. 4

    Stay connected

    Come to events, give back when you can, and help the next person who arrives.

Your voice

Services help one person. A collective voice helps everyone.

Some problems cannot be solved one coffee at a time, because they sit in the rules. So InDe members approved an eight point Policy Agenda, and we carry it to the institutions and policymakers who can remove the barriers for good. When you join, that voice gets a little stronger.

Approved by InDe members, May 2026.

  • 01 A national integration strategy
  • 02 An independent body to monitor progress
  • 03 International representation in policymaking
  • 04 Fair treatment in the labour market
  • 05 Access to work for spouses and partners
  • 06 Fewer barriers and costs to renew residence
  • 07 Public services you can use in a language you read
  • 08 A real housing strategy

Good to know

Questions people ask first.

Is InDe membership free?

Yes. Anyone can join free and belongs from day one, with full access to the community and events. Silver and Platinum add deeper one to one support, and you start with a free call. No cost to talk, and no pressure to decide.

Who can join InDe?

Every international who makes Denmark home, of any nationality and in any city, and the Danes who welcome them. If that is you, you belong here.

What is a Denmark Buddy?

A real person who has done it before, matched to you, to help you settle. Part guide, part friend, part local ambassador.

What does Building now mean?

Some services are live today and some are still being built. Each service tells you which, so you always know exactly what you can use right now.

How is InDe funded?

The community runs on people, not fees. Most of what InDe does is delivered by volunteers and funded by partner organisations, which is how membership stays free for everyone.

Denmark is better when internationals stay.

Membership is free, the help is real, and you are not doing this alone. Join InDe today, and the next person who arrives will not have to do it alone either.
