What membership unlocks

Four ways InDe stands with you.

Building a life in a new country is not one task. It is many, and they come in waves. InDe is built in four pillars that follow your life here, from your first week to leading where you are. Each service shows the level that opens it, and whether it is live now or building now.

Platinum Our flagship

Meet your Denmark Buddy.

Settling in is rarely about the paperwork. It is the hundred small things no one tells you. A Denmark Buddy is a real person who has been new here too, matched to you for the part that is hard to do alone. You get someone in your corner, so you can ask the awkward questions without feeling stupid.

  1. 1
    Get matched

    We pair you with a buddy near you who fits your situation, your field, and your language.

  2. 2
    Meet up

    A coffee, a walk, or a call. They answer the real questions and show you how things actually work here.

  3. 3
    Find your footing

    A tour of your neighbourhood, help with one thing that has you stuck, and a friendly face through your first months.

Get a Denmark Buddy Buddies are InDe Supporters who give their time. Want to help the next person who arrives? Become a buddy.

How it works, in four steps
